About The Firm

Two Decades of Excellence in
Chartered Accountancy

Chetan Agarwal & Co. is a professional Chartered Accountants firm delivering specialized services in taxation, audit, GST, business advisory, compliance, and litigation support.

The firm is led by CA Dipak Dama, FCA, having 17+ years of experience in complex income tax matters, faceless scrutiny assessments, reassessment proceedings, appellate representation, and high-stakes tax litigation. CA Dipak Dama is known for analytical precision, technically robust drafting, strategic representation, and deep command over income tax law, procedural compliance, and judicial precedents.

The firm provides result-oriented advisory services to businesses, professionals, and individuals facing sophisticated tax and compliance challenges — from initial notice to final appellate relief.
